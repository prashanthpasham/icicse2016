Sessions

All papers that conform to submission guidelines will be peer reviewed and evaluated based on originality, technical and/or research content/depth, correctness, relevance to conference, contributions, and readability. The manuscripts should be submitted in PDF format in above links. Acceptance of papers will be communicated to authors by Chair. 
At least one full paying author of each accepted paper must register for the conference before the indicated deadline. All the accepted papers to be presented in this session will be published in special issue of "Journal of Innovation in Computer Science and Engineering (JICSE) with ISSN 2278-0947 (www.innovationjournals.com).
Our special sessions are not limited to the above sessions. Researchers and Academician and Technologists are  invited to propose Special Sessions (SS) keeping parity with modern era. You will notified within 15 days of submission of proposal to us. All papers of Special Sessions has be submitted through the ICICSE 2016 above LINKs and reviewed with the same review process.
