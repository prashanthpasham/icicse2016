Registration

The conference registration covers conference kit, coffee breaks, lunch and banquet. The registration doesn’t cover accommodation, transportation etc. Free pooled transport service will be provided from designated accommodation to campus for outstation delegates.

All accepted papers will be published in the prestigious Springer AISC (Advances in Intelligent Systems and Computing) Series. At least one author of an accepted paper is required to register at the full registration rate. If an author has got more than one accepted papers, each paper has also to be registered.
